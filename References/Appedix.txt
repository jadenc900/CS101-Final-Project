arrivalDate - date of landing in China
deadline24hr - the calculated deadline for the 24hr form based on date of arrival
permit_date - 1 month from arriaval date
res_permit_deadline - calculated last day to have an appointment scheduled at the 
						exit entry bureau (1 day before 1 month from arrival)
health_check_date - ISS recommended date to schedule a health check
health_check_deadline - Last day to have a health check and receive the report
						before the last day to have a permit appointment
simdate - ISS recommended date to set up a sim card/phone number
bankdate - ISS recommended date to set up a bank account
health_insurance - date to submit health insurance form (3 days before arrival)
